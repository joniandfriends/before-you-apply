We are excited for you to apply to a Family Retreat!  There are a few important details to know before you start your application...

Please be prepared to complete everyone’s application in one sitting. You will not be able to save your information and return later. Please complete a separate application for each individual interested in attending.

The first application should be for the primary attendee/Head of Household/the Parent or Legal Guardian or Primary Caregiver of the family, who is 18 years or older.

Additional attendees’ applications should be shorter. It is intended that the Head of Household will be the person completing all applications.

Payment is not required to apply. Once you are approved to attend the event, you will be given the cost information. You will then be able to make payments and/or start fundraising.

The Head of Household can apply for a Personal Care Attendant at the time that they apply for the whole family. Or, if the Head of Household wishes for the Personal Care Attendant to apply on their own later, the Head of Household will need to provide the Personal Care Attendant with their family name as stated on the application.

Ready to apply? Here's a checklist to help you.

Please take a minute to collect all the information listed below for you and each person applying to attend the event with you.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

  • Name, date of birth, gender, marital status, and occupation
  • Contact information: mailing address and email address
  • At least one phone number: home, work or mobile is required
  • Role in family (Mother, Father, Spouse, Friend, etc.)
  • T-Shirt Size (Please take into consideration our t-shirts run on the smaller side).
  • Language: first language and any others you use for communication
  • How you heard about Joni and Friends
  • Church name (if applicable)
  • Two (2) Emergency Contacts: name, email address, and phone number for the family unit
  • Information about Personal Care Attendant: first and last name at a minimum

MEDICAL INFORMATION

  • Important information related to any disability, or medical condition(s) you may have
  • Any special accommodations required
  • List of any medication, medical equipment, treatments
  • List of allergies (seasonal, medication, food, etc.)
  • Insurance policy carrier and policy number
  • Physician information: name, phone number, and address

This application is for individuals only.

You will not be given the opportunity to add other people or a Personal Care Attendant to the application. If you wish you attend with other people, please apply as a family or group.

Payment is not required to apply. Once you are approved to attend the event, you will be given the cost information. You will then be able to make payments and/or start fundraising.
